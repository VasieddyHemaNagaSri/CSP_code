Aloe Vera

Required Temp:20-30°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Heat Stress: High heat disrupts the plant's growth and development.
Sunburn: Leaves develop white or brown spots from excess sun.

Basil

Required Temp:21-32°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Leaf Scorch: Leaves turn brown due to excessive sunlight exposure.
Heat Stress: High heat disrupts the plant's growth and development.

Peppermint

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated

Leaf burn: Leaves may dry out and become brittle.
Stunted growth: Excess heat can inhibit growth and cause the plant to wilt.

Ashwagandha

Required Temp:21-35°C

Diseases if Overheated

Leaf Burn: Leaves turn brown due to excessive sunlight exposure.
Root Rot: Overheated roots decay, leading to plant death.

Ginger

Required Temp:21-32°C

Diseases if Overheated

Root Rot: Overheated roots decay, leading to plant death.
Yellowing: Leaves lose color, indicating stress and damage.
