Water Lilies

Required Temp:18-29°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Algae Overgrowth: Excessive algae cover reduces light.
Root Rot: Fungal decay of roots due to heat.

Duckweed

Required Temp:21-29°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Algal Blooms: Overgrowth of algae affects water quality.
Nutrient Deficiency: Poor growth due to imbalanced nutrients.

Cattails

Required Temp:21-29°C

Diseases if Overheated

Leaf Spot Disease: Dark spots on leaves from fungal infections.
Root Decay: Poor oxygenation leads to root decay.

Eelgrass

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated

Brown Algae: Algae growth smothers eelgrass and blocks light.
Heat Stress: Damage and dieback from prolonged heat.

Hornwort

Required Temp:15-24°C

Diseases if Overheated

Algae Infestation: High temperatures promote algae growth on plants.
Nutrient Imbalance: Excess heat disrupts nutrient uptake.

Java Moss

Required Temp:18-27°C

Diseases if Overheated

Brown Spots: High temperatures cause discoloration and decay.
Algae Growth: Excessive heat encourages algae on moss.
