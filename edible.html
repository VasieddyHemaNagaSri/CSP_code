Tomatoes

Required Temp:21-29°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Blossom End Rot: Dark spots on fruit bottoms due to uneven watering and heat.
Fusarium Wilt: Leaves yellow and wilt, worsened by high temperatures.

Lettuce

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Bolting: Early seed production causing bitter leaves due to high temperatures.
Downy Mildew: Yellowing leaves with mold, aggravated by warm, moist conditions.

Peppers

Required Temp:21-29°C

Diseases if Overheated

Sunscald: White patches on fruits from too much heat and direct sunlight.
Bacterial Spot: Dark spots on leaves and fruit, intensified by high temperatures.

Zucchini

Required Temp:21-29°C

Diseases if Overheated

Powdery Mildew: White fungal growth on leaves, exacerbated by warm, dry weather.
Bacterial Wilt: Leaf wilting and yellowing, worse in heat.

spinach

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated

Downy Mildew: Yellow leaves with mold, worsened by heat.
Bolting: Early seeding, causing bitter leaves from high temperatures.

Strawberries

Required Temp:15-27°C

Diseases if Overheated

Gray Mold (Botrytis): Fuzzy gray mold on berries, worsened by warmth.
Leaf Scorch: Brown, dry leaf patches, intensified by heat.
