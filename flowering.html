Roses

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Powdery Mildew: White, powdery spots on leaves. Manage with fungicides and spacing.
Black Spot: Dark, circular spots on leaves. Improve air circulation and use fungicides

Petunias

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated:

Botrytis Blight: Gray mold on flowers and leaves. Improve ventilation and avoid overhead watering.
Aphid Infestation: Distorted leaves. Control with insecticidal soap.

Marigold

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated

Rust: Orange pustules on leaves. Remove infected leaves and use fungicides.
Downy Mildew: Yellowing leaves with white mold. Improve air circulation and apply fungicides.

Spider

Required Temp:18-24°C

Diseases if Overheated

Leaf Tips Browning: Due to high temperatures or low humidity. Increase humidity and adjust temperature.
Root Rot: Caused by overwatering. Ensure well-draining soil and adjust watering.

Ferns

Required Temp:15-21°C

Diseases if Overheated

Bud Rot: Wilting and browning of central leaves. Improve air circulation and avoid excess moisture.
Leaf Spot Disease: Dark spots on leaves. Use fungicides and ensure good drainage.

Palms

Required Temp:21-29°C

Diseases if Overheated

Bud Rot: Wilting and browning of central leaves. Improve air circulation and avoid excess moisture.
Leaf Spot Disease: Dark spots on leaves. Use fungicides and ensure good drainage.
